Core Features

Everything your freight
team needs in one place

From job creation to final invoice — FreightERP handles the entire shipment lifecycle without missing a step.

📋
Complete Job Management
Create, track, and close FCL, LCL, and AIR shipment jobs. Manage BL details, HBL/MBL numbers, consignee info, and container details — all in one screen.
💰
Smart Cost & Invoicing
Automatically calculate receivables, payables, and net profit per job. Generate professional invoices and credit notes with multi-currency support.
📦
Pre-Shipment Tracking
Track pre-shipment jobs with ETA alerts. Get automatic 8-day advance notifications when shipments are due to arrive — never miss a critical deadline.
📊
Global Reports & Analytics
Generate job-wise profit reports, container status summaries, and revenue dashboards. Filter by date range and export to your team instantly.
🌐
LAN Multi-PC Network
Connect multiple computers on your office network to a single shared database. All team members work on the same live data — no sync required.
🔐
Role-Based Access Control
Admin and staff roles with separate permissions. Sensitive data like revenue and accounting is visible only to admins, keeping operations secure.
